Background

The Sol Pact Navy evacuated Sylvia's home planet Idone, which was at risk of being consumed by a black hole, when she was 23. She was inspired to help others and joined the Navy at 25. She graduated at 29 with a bachelor of Public Health and finished medical school at 33. She was then assigned to the Arcturus.
