Background

Born into a noble family on a feudal outer rim planet, Perth did not ever find it necessary to work one day in her life. However, with tensions between other kingdoms and her homeland rising, her parents found it necessary that she learn proper work ethic and to build her own future if her nation was to fall. As such, they decided she could choose her own path best suited for what she wishes to achieve. After deciding on a career in engineering for the military, she signed aboard a Sol Pact Navy ship, as it seemed to be safest off the planet than anywhere else to preserve the bloodline.
